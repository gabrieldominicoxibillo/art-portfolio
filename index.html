About Myself


I am an artist with a passion for programming. Over the past thirteen years, I have immersed myself in various forms of Graphic Design, discerning elements that pique my interest and those that do not. Gradually, I have developed a keen eye for aesthetics and a knack for creating visually engaging designs that resonate with audiences. My experience covers designing Logos, Websites, Web Banners, Content Marketing Materials, Animations, 3D Modeling, Illustrations, Infographics, PowerPoint Presentations, Display Ads, Print or Digital Drochures, T-shirt Graphics, Packaging & Labels, Banner Ads for Google, Facebook, Social Media, etc., Social media graphics, Promotional items, Outdoor graphics, Stickers, Email Header Graphics & Signatures, Blog header graphics, “Hero” images, photo edits & photo manipulation, Removing backgrounds from images, E-commerce graphics, Webpage Design, GIF animations, Merchandise, Presentation Graphic Elements, Video Editing, Presentation Designs, Signages, Photorealism, Game Assets, Portraits, Medical Illustrations, Coloring Books, Flyers, Video Editing, Visual Effects (VFX), and Posters, all of which complement my programming skills when crafting websites. Primarily, I paint and manipulate backgrounds using various creative software such as Adobe Creative Suite, while leveraging my 3D skills to incorporate additional elements. My attention to detail extends to the pixel level, a trait crucial in programming. Proficient in both traditional and digital illustration techniques, I bring concepts to life with creativity and precision. I am resourceful, continuously learning new tools to enhance output quality while meeting tight deadlines. With my versatile skill set and collaborative spirit, I am confident in my ability to contribute significantly to any creative team.
